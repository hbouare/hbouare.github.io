Development of an online lottery management platform enabling users to participate in digital draws through a secure and transparent system.

The platform allows users to create accounts, purchase lottery tickets, participate in scheduled draws, and access the complete history of their participation and results. A secure randomization engine ensures fairness and integrity during the draw process.

An administrative interface enables operators to configure lottery campaigns, define participation rules, manage draw schedules, publish results, and monitor ticket sales and transaction activity. The system also provides analytics dashboards to track participation metrics and campaign performance.
